Satsang Exams

In the evening assembly in Robbinsville, on September 15, 2017, Mahant Swami Maharaj was asked, "With what understanding and glory should we appear for the Satsang Exam?"

"Yogi Bapa had talked often to Ishwarcharan Swami about setting up an infrastructure for Satsang Exams. Shriji Maharaj values this a lot but we do not value it because the Satsang Exam certificate is of no use, unlike the academic certificate we attain. We simply give the Satsang Exams for sake of it. But one attains moksha, which no one can give.”
